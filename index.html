About Wellgivers

Wellgivers is a healthcare sponsorship platform that bridges the gap between sponsors and beneficiaries. We empower sponsors to support their loved ones, employees, or communities by making healthcare contributions easy, transparent, and trackable.

Every sponsorship provides beneficiaries with access to quality healthcare through our HMO plan.
